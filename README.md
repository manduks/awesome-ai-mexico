## Free AI Tools

Tools specifically designed for Mexican and Latin American businesses.

### Business Tools
- [RooxAI Scanner](https://www.rooxai.com/es/scanner) — Analyze any website's AI readiness. Get a score (0-100), grade, and specific opportunities.
- [RooxAI Chatbot Generator](https://www.rooxai.com/es/try) — Generate an AI chatbot for your business in 30 seconds. No coding, no signup. 22 industries supported.
- [Generador de Aviso de Privacidad](https://www.rooxai.com/es/herramientas/aviso-de-privacidad) — Generate a LFPDPPP-compliant privacy notice for Mexican businesses.
- [Calculadora de Ahorro con IA](https://www.rooxai.com/es/herramientas/calculadora-ahorro-ia) — Calculate how much your business could save by implementing AI.
- [Generador de Prompts](https://www.rooxai.com/es/herramientas/generador-prompts) — AI prompt generator optimized for business use cases.

### Communication Tools
- [Plantillas de WhatsApp Business](https://www.rooxai.com/es/herramientas/plantillas-whatsapp) — Professional WhatsApp message templates for businesses.
- [Responder Reseñas de Google con IA](https://www.rooxai.com/es/herramientas/responder-resenas) — Generate professional responses to Google reviews using AI.
- [Calculadora de Riesgo IA en Trabajo](https://www.rooxai.com/es/herramientas/riesgo-ia-trabajo) — Check how AI will impact your job/profession.

### General Purpose
- [ChatGPT](https://chat.openai.com) — OpenAI's conversational AI. Free tier available.
- [Claude](https://claude.ai) — Anthropic's AI assistant. Excellent for Spanish language tasks.
- [Gemini](https://gemini.google.com) — Google's AI assistant with search integration.
- [Perplexity](https://perplexity.ai) — AI search engine with citations.

## Open Source

### Frameworks
- [LangChain](https://github.com/langchain-ai/langchain) — Framework for building LLM applications.
- [LlamaIndex](https://github.com/run-llama/llama_index) — Data framework for LLM applications.
- [Flowise](https://github.com/FlowiseAI/Flowise) — Visual LLM flow builder.

### Models
- [Llama 3](https://github.com/meta-llama/llama3) — Meta's open source LLM.
- [Mistral](https://mistral.ai) — European open-weight models.
- [Qwen](https://github.com/QwenLM/Qwen) — Alibaba's multilingual model (good Spanish support).

### Chat Interfaces
