# Contributing to Awesome AI Mexico

Thanks for your interest in contributing! This list is curated to help businesses in Mexico and Latin America discover useful AI tools and resources.

## How to Contribute

1. Fork this repository
2. Add your suggestion in the appropriate category
3. Use the format: `[Tool Name](URL) — Brief description.`
4. Submit a pull request

## Guidelines

- The tool/resource should be relevant to businesses in Mexico or Latin America
- Free tools or tools with free tiers are preferred
- Include a brief (1-2 sentence) description
- Spanish-language resources are especially welcome
- No affiliate links

## Categories

If your suggestion doesn't fit an existing category, feel free to propose a new one.

## Questions?

Open an issue or contact us at hello@rooxai.com.
